ShiftPulse

Your Shift. Your Pulse. Your Pay.

Built by a Flight Nurse for Medical Professionals

Features

  • Fatigue Tracking: Real-time wellness score based on your sleep, circadian rhythm, and hours awake
  • Shift Scheduling: Rotating patterns, calendar views (Day/Week/Month/Year), pattern management
  • Pay Calculator: Overtime, double time, shift differentials, anti-pyramiding, holiday multipliers
  • Alertness Check: Assess your alertness before driving home after a long shift
  • Apple Watch: Fatigue score and shift info on your wrist with complications
  • 401k Tracker: Track retirement contributions with IRS limit awareness
  • 100% Private: All data stays on your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking.
